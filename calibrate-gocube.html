🎮 GoCube Move Calibration

This tool will help calibrate your GoCube's accelerometer data to specific cube moves, just like configuring a game controller!

📡 Disconnected - Connect your GoCube to begin

📋 Calibration Instructions

  1. Connect your GoCube using the "Connect GoCube" button
  2. Click "Start Calibration" to begin the process
  3. For each highlighted move, perform that exact move when prompted
  4. The system will record the accelerometer signature for each move
  5. After all moves are calibrated, you'll get a mapping table to use in the main app
